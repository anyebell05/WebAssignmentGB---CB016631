Breathing Techniques

Explore various techniques to enhance your breathing practice and promote relaxation.

4-7-8 Breathing

Inhale for 4 seconds, hold for 7, and exhale for 8. A great method to calm the mind before sleep.

Box Breathing

Breathe in for 4, hold for 4, exhale for 4, and hold again for 4. Ideal for stress relief and focus.

Diaphragmatic Breathing

Focus on deep belly breaths to increase oxygen flow, reduce stress, and improve relaxation.
